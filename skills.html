My Major Projects

Strokes By Chemz, 2021

Aesthetically done for a calligraphy artist in Dubai, the website showcases the different watercolor and lettering works created by her.

KeyPlus Locking Solutions, 2021

Website for a keys and locks shop in Kerala, listing the different kinds of services available, and featuring the works done for satisfied customers.

Timmy Cross the Road, 2021

A Python GUI game in which the player must help Timmy the turtle cross a road full of (randomly generated) oncoming cars. The game levels up when the turtle reaches the other side, and the speed of the oncoming traffic increases on each level. The game is built on the concept of Object-Oriented Programming.

Landmark World Residents’ App, 2020

A Flutter app that consolidates all the details of the residents of Landmark World, an apartment complex in Kerala. Residents’ details can be searched based on several factors such as apartment numbers or car registration numbers, but can only be modified by authorized admins.

Weekenders’ Pick-A-Path game, 2020

A simple game developed in Flutter. The player has to navigate through a pair of choices for each premise, and the outcome of the game will be based on these choices.

ZNM EMI Calculator, 2020

An EMI calculator app developed in Flutter for a local money-dealing team. The app calculates the EMI for each month based on the principal amount and the kind of loan, for a specified number of years.

Al Hilal College Management Software, 2010

A web-based software developed in C#.NET to consolidate the details of the students of Al Hilal College in Sharjah. Administrators could check upon the academic, transportation and financial records and reports of students. The software was later withdrawn due to the college's financial reasons.

Security Implementation Using Biometrics, 2009

Main Project in University. The software developed involved reading biometrics (thumbprints) through a scanner and analysing them for logging into email services. Thumbprints were required for sending as well as receiving emails.
